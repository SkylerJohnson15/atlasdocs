Acme Co - Support Knowledge Base

PASSWORD RESET
To reset your password, click “Forgot Password” on the login page. You will receive an email link. The link expires after 15 minutes.

ACCOUNT ACCESS
If you cannot access your account, confirm your email is correct and try resetting your password. If the problem continues, contact support.

BILLING
Billing occurs on the 1st of every month. You can update your payment method from the Billing section in your account settings.

REFUNDS
Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase for annual plans only. Monthly plans are not refundable. Refunds take 5-7 business days.

TECHNICAL ISSUES
If the app is crashing, update to the latest version. If issues continue, reinstall the app and restart your device.
